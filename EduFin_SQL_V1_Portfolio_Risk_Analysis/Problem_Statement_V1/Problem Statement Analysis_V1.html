Version 1 Crisis Analysis

CEO Emergency Directive:
"We've discovered critical discrepancies in our loan portfolio that could impact our Q3 financial reporting and regulatory compliance. I need our best analyst to investigate immediately and provide actionable insights within 48 hours. The board meeting is Monday, and we need concrete answers about our exposure and mitigation strategies."

— Sarah Chen, CEO, EduFin

Crisis Scenario Details

  • Discovery: Automated audit reveals $12M discrepancy in reported vs. actual loan disbursements
  • Timeline: Issue potentially affecting 6 months of transactions
  • Impact: 15,000+ student accounts potentially affected
  • Regulatory Risk: Federal compliance audit scheduled for next month
  • Business Impact: Potential credit rating implications and stakeholder confidence

Critical Analysis Questions

Question 1: Loan Portfolio Assessment

  • What is the total outstanding loan amount across all active loans?
  • Identify the top 10 highest-risk loans based on amount and repayment history
  • Calculate average loan amounts by loan type and student credit score ranges
  • Determine which institution partnerships generate the highest loan volumes

Question 2: Payment Performance Analysis

  • Calculate monthly payment collection rates over the past 12 months
  • Identify students with missed payments exceeding $1,000
  • Analyze payment method preferences and their success rates
  • Determine seasonal trends in payment behavior

Question 3: Student Demographics & Performance

  • Profile student segments by enrollment patterns and financial behavior
  • Identify correlation between credit scores and loan performance
  • Analyze geographic distribution and regional performance variations
  • Determine optimal loan amounts based on student profiles

Question 4: Institutional Partnership Analysis

  • Evaluate partnership tier performance and ROI
  • Identify institutions with highest default rates
  • Calculate revenue per institution and partnership efficiency
  • Recommend partnership optimization strategies

Question 5: Data Quality & Integrity

  • Identify data anomalies and inconsistencies across tables
  • Validate referential integrity and business rule compliance
  • Detect duplicate records and data entry errors
  • Recommend data governance improvements
